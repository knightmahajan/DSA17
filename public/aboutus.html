DIRECTORATE OF STUDENT AFFAIRS - Transcending Limits


The Directorate of Student Affairs is a body that is run for the students and by the students of SRM University, Kattankulathur. Meant for everything cultural on campus, the Directorate comprises of multiple domains, clubs and houses. It organizes various events throughout the year to improve cultural integrity and break the monotony of lectures and labs.

The very first event organised by DSA at the beginning of every odd semester is Shuru. Held exclusively for the freshers of the university, Shuru is one’s big shot to fame. More importantly, it provides a great opportunity for the newcomers to meet people with similar interests and take a step closer towards making themselves at home.

Team spirit and sportsmanship are qualities every individual must possess. Jhalak and Tarana are two spectacular inter-house festivals that celebrate culture, art and healthy competitive spirit. Road shows with snazzy banners and flash mobs mark the approach of these festivals. They comprise of a variety of competitions over a wide array of domains. Providing an amazing platform for all students to showcase their talent, these are two of the most anticipated events of the year. Talking about inter-house events, one cannot simply miss out on Khel. A sporting event like no other, Khel is both figuratively and literally a battlefield. The competition is immense, the field of play is immersive and the players get a first-hand experience of the competition and raw talent lying dormant inside each and every one of them.

Taking place at the start of every new year is SRM's most exuberant cultural festival, Milan. Being one of South India's premier college festivals, Milan sets itself apart from other fests with a plethora of events across various domains. From journalism and photography to gaming and quizzes, garnering support from various esteemed institutions and graced by a multitude of celebrities and exponents from the world over, Milan is a tantalising experience that leaves every individual buzzing with energy.

Another one of SRM’s most celebrated events is Yuva. Held every year in the month of August, Yuva commemorates the birthday of the founder of the University. With events going on round the clock for the entire day and exciting cash prizes, this event provides a unique experience to all the participants and organizers. Apart from the festivities, the Directorate also plays a role in the enhancement of the society, with events targeted at the betterment of the underprivileged individuals and the environment. Butterflies is one such event wherein underprivileged children from multiple schools around the campus are invited to spend a day in the university, involving all sorts of extra-curricular and fun activities.
